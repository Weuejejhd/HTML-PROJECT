Our Services

Camera Repairing

This service involves a thorough cleaning of the camera and lenses, followed by lubrication of moving parts. It can help improve the camera's functionality and extend its lifespan.

Shutter Replacement

If the camera's shutter is not functioning properly or is completely broken, replacing it is essential. This service involves removing the old shutter and installing a new one, calibrated to the correct speed for the camera.

Clean and Lubricate

Over time, the foam or felt light seals inside a vintage camera can deteriorate and become ineffective. This can result in light leaks and damage to your photos. Replacing these seals is a quick and affordable service that can greatly improve the camera's performance.
