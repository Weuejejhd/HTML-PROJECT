More About Us

Who Are Our Technicians?

Our team of skilled technicians have decades of experience in repairing and restoring vintage cameras, and

we are passionate about preserving these beautiful pieces of photographic history for generations to come.

What's our understandings about camera?

We understand that vintage cameras hold a special place in the hearts of photographers and collectors alike, and that each camera has its own unique story to tell.
That's why we approach each repair with the utmost care and attention to detail, ensuring that every camera is restored to its original condition and functionality.

About our specialisation in vintage camera?

We specialize in repairing a wide variety of vintage cameras, from classic film cameras to antique wooden view cameras. Our expertise extends to all types of repairs, from simple fixes like replacing a shutter to more complex issues like repairing a damaged lens or shutter mechanism.

Why choose us?

We take great pride in our work and are committed to providing the highest quality repairs and customer service possible.

We believe that our work speaks for itself, and we are confident that you will be thrilled with the results of our repairs.
